## Review Checklist

- The eight shapes are distinguishable at 128 x 128.
- Heart, Diamond and Inverted Triangular read as believable human face contours.
- Ears, neck, baseline, stroke weight and padding feel consistent.
- Runtime slices have transparent background and no white halo.
- Selected-state recolor works through CSS mask mode.
