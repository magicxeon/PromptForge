## First Pass

The first pass maps reviewed expression attributes:

- `expression.001` Subtle Micro-Expression
- `expression.002` Slightly Thoughtful
- `expression.004` Soft Friendly Smile
- `expression.003` Candid Gentle Laugh
- `expression.008` Playful Mild Smirk
- `expression.010` Reflective Mood

Review focus:

- Mood should be readable at thumbnail size.
- Head pose, face shape, and camera should stay fixed.
- No hair, beard, makeup, or gendered styling should be encoded in the icons.
- Expression options that imply head tilt are excluded from this pilot.
