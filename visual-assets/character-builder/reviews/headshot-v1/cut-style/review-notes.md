## Presentation-Aware Pass

The first presentation-aware pass maps the reviewed hairstyle/cut attributes:

- `hair_008` Ponytail
- `hair_009` Messy Bun
- `hair_010` French Braid
- `hair_022` Layered Hush Cut
- `hair_023` Long Loose Waves
- `hair_024` Side-swept Hair
- `hair_025` Wet Look Hair
- `hair_026` Wolf Cut
- `hair_029` Crew Cut
- `hair_030` Side Part
- `hair_031` Undercut
- `hair_032` Pompadour
- `hair_033` Quiff
- `hair_034` Textured Crop
- `hair_035` Caesar Cut
- `hair_036` Short Curly Crop

Rows 1-2 are feminine/unisex options for the first MVP pass. Rows 3-4 are masculine options. `Wet Look Hair` and `Wolf Cut` remain available in the masculine set because they can work as neutral/editorial styles.

Excluded from this visual set:

- `hair_005` Straight Hair
- `hair_006` Wavy Hair
- `hair_007` Curly Hair

These excluded options belong to the hair texture axis and should be handled separately.

Review focus:

- Each cut/style should be distinguishable at thumbnail size.
- Icons should avoid encoding hair color.
- Face, expression, and skin details should remain absent.
