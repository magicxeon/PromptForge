## First Pass

The first pass uses the existing 7 eyebrow attributes and keeps the source sheet as a 1x7 row to avoid adding a visual-only placeholder.

Review focus:

- Straight, soft arched, thin, and natural thick should be clearly distinguishable at thumbnail size.
- Defined, well-groomed, and natural may need later art direction because their semantic difference is subtle.
- The set must remain eyebrow-only and must not include eyes, skin, makeup, or expression.
