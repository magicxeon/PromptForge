# Nose Review Notes

## Source

- `visual-assets/character-builder/source-sets/headshot-v1/facial-features/nose/nose-set-r1.png`

## First Pass

The first pass uses the existing 6 nose attributes and keeps the source sheet as a 1x6 row.

## Revision Notes

- `r1` was regenerated after review because the first source sheet made the nose bridges too tall.
- The current source follows a compact reference style with shorter bridge marks and lower overall bridge height.
- `high-bridge` remains intentionally taller than the other variants, but should still read as compact rather than a long fashion-sketch nose.

Review focus:

- Small button, high bridge, delicate narrow, and straight should be distinguishable at thumbnail size.
- Soft rounded tip and natural may need later art direction because their semantic difference can be subtle.
- The set must remain nose-only and must not include eyes, lips, skin, makeup, or expression.
