# Face Shape Source Sheet Prompt

Use the shared illustrated-set master prompt with:

- FIELD_LABEL: Face Shape
- COLUMNS: 4
- ROWS: 2
- VISUAL_AXIS: the outer face contour, jaw width, cheekbone width and chin taper

Order:

1. Oval
2. Square
3. Round
4. Diamond
5. Rectangular
6. Heart
7. Inverted Triangular
8. Long

Anatomy constraints:

- Oval: balanced softly elongated face with gently curved jaw.
- Square: broad forehead and jaw with softened human corners.
- Round: shorter, fuller cheek area with a softly rounded jaw.
- Diamond: cheekbone area is widest while forehead and jaw are narrower.
- Rectangular: longer face with straighter sides and a broader jaw than oval.
- Heart: broader upper face tapering through the jaw to a soft narrow chin.
- Inverted Triangular: broader upper face tapering to a narrower jaw and chin.
- Long: elongated human face with natural proportions, not a capsule symbol.

Do not draw literal heart, diamond, square, rectangle or triangle symbols.
