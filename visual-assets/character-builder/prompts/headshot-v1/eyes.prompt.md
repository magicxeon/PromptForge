# Eyes Source Sheet Prompt

Use the shared illustrated-set master prompt with:

- FIELD_LABEL: Eyes
- COLUMNS: 4
- ROWS: 2
- VISUAL_AXIS: eye opening shape, eyelid crease, outer-corner direction and upper-lid coverage

Order:

1. Almond
2. Monolid
3. Double Eyelids
4. Round
5. Phoenix
6. Doe
7. Puppy
8. Hooded

Anatomy constraints:

- Show one symmetrical pair of human eyes per cell.
- Keep gaze straight forward and neutral in every cell.
- Keep iris/pupil size, eyebrow absence, makeup absence, line weight, baseline and scale consistent.
- Almond: tapered inner and outer corners with balanced moderate opening.
- Monolid: smooth upper lid without a visible crease.
- Double Eyelids: clear natural upper eyelid crease above the lash line.
- Round: taller open eye shape with rounded upper and lower lids.
- Phoenix: elegant elongated eyes with gently lifted outer corners.
- Doe: soft open rounded eyes, gentle and innocent but still neutral.
- Puppy: soft eyes with slightly lowered outer corners, not sad or crying.
- Hooded: upper lid partially covers the crease and top of the eye opening.

Do not add eyebrows, eyelashes, makeup, tears, emotion symbols, labels, text, face outline, nose, mouth, shadows, gradients or colored iris details.
