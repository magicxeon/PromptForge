# Task attr-007: Camera Settings, Lighting & Environment Presets

## 1. Goal
Configure technical camera specifications and dramatic environmental lighting presets that reflect photorealistic parameters.

## 2. Attribute Specifications

*   **Camera Settings**:
    *   `Modern mirrorless camera`: Clean high-fidelity digital image look.
    *   `50mm lens`: Natural portrait lens geometry.
    *   `f/1.4 or f/1.8`: Extremely shallow depth of field.
    *   `Shallow depth of field`: Soft background/foreground bokeh.
    *   `Slightly low angle`: Flattering perspective.
*   **Lighting Settings**:
    *   `Strong flash lighting`: Direct hard-flash that highlights skin sheen and reflections.
    *   `Red and pink neon ambient`: Soft dramatic glow casting rich red-pink color tones.
    *   `Neon highlights`: Bright specular rims on hair strands and skin edge outlines.
*   **Environment Presets**:
    *   `Vibrant crowded nightclub / bar`: Atmospheric indoor nightclub backdrops.
    *   `Props`: Green glass bottle, glass with purple straw, smartphone, table drink.
    *   `Out of focus background`: Blurred figures of other people under neon ambient light.
